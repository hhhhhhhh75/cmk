Cascade Mountain Boarding Services

Dogs receive:

• A stay on a beautiful farm.

• An opportunity to see and socialize with our kids and many kinds of animals (as seen on the slideshow).

• A family of Bernese Mountain Dogs to interact and play with.

• Fully-fenced in areas to run and play.

• Quality care and attention.

• Our facilities have video surveillance.

• We are certified by the City of Abbotsford and by the SPCA animal shelter.
