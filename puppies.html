Quito

Quito is an amazing Berner! We imported him as a pup from Europe. He has numerous championship titles in his lineage including international Championships. His 4 generation pedigree averages 9 year of age. He has many relatives that have lived for over 10 years. He is the perfect male Bernese Mountain dog. He has a large frame, weighing in at 125 lbs and strong bone. He has perfect markings and a great temperament â€“ friendly, great with people, loyal, affectionate and outgoing. Karl

Karl also comes from a long list of champions including International and World Champions. He is the son of Champion Quito and Ella. He is a beautiful male Bernese Mountain dog. He is very similar in size to his father Quito. He has big bone and a big structure. His beautiful looks and amazing personality gave him the opportunity to be in few episodes in the television series "The Babysitters Club". He is very friendly and outgoing. He loves to cuddle and sit at your feet. He is an ideal Berner!

Females

Mila

Mila is a puppy we kept from one of our previous litters. Her mother is Bailey, and her grandmother is McKenzie, who is now retired. Her father is Champion Jago. She has a medium sized build, but strong bone structure. Her coat is a medium length and silky. She has a very gentle and loving personality, and is one of our more laid back females. She is the perfect companion dog!

Nova

Nova is from Champion European bloodlines. We held her and her sister Misha back. She is the daughter of McKenzie and Champion Jago. Mckenzie and her mother Zoey have both reached close to 12 years of age. She has a great temperament, and is sweet natured. She dearly loves those she knows, but can be unsure of people she doesn't know at first. She loves to go and grab a leash when it is time to go for a walk. She has a smaller frame and good bone. Her markings are perfect. She is an awesome female.
