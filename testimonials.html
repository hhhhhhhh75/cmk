Testimonials

Our family's journey with Cascade Mountain Bernese Mountain Dogs began nearly 9 years ago when we welcomed one into our home. In our search for the ideal puppy, we visited several breeders, but our tour of Cascade stood out. It was evident that every animal there was cherished and well-cared for. Gerhardt's commitment to the well-being and happiness of his breeding stock is truly remarkable. His meticulous attention to detail ensures the safety and health of both mother and pups. We were fortunate to be matched with the perfect puppy who quickly became an integral part of our family, forming a special bond with my boys. Words cannot adequately express our gratitude to Cascade for blessing us with such a wonderful companion. Brylie, our beloved dog, is now 9 years young and continues to bring joy and happiness into our lives.

When we were looking for a Bernese Mountain dog, we researched several breeders. We feel forunate to have found Cascade Mountain Kennel; they provided extensive information about their dogs, breeding practices and characteristics of the Bernes Mountain Dog. It is obvious that they take pride in their work and are concerned about the dogs’ welfare. Mila, our current Berner is our 3 rd dog from Cascade. She joined our family when she was 12 weeks old and she is such a fun, loyal and loving dog. What stands out for us is how well-socialized she was when we picked her up. She is careful around our small grandchildren, gets along with other dogs and is sweet and curious about other animals including cats. Whenever we travel, Mila goes back to the farm for boarding. She loves palying and hanging out with the other Berners and we know that she is well taken care of while we are away. We trust Gerhard and his staff, he is an experienced and responsible breeder and highly recommend him to anyone who is thinking about adopting a Berner.

We’ve known Gerhard and his family at Cascade Mountain Kennels since 2014 when we adopted our first Berner, Ketchum. Cascade Mountain Kennels came highly recommended, and they matched us with the perfect new family member. Ketchum had the best temperament and was so great with his human siblings that later came along. We recently welcomed Hailey to the family, and while she has big shoes to fill, she’s such a sweetie and adapting well. Highly recommend Gerhard and Cascade Mountain Kennels.
